This repository includes:
1) EV datasets of Luohu District (corresponding to IEEE-33) of Shenzhen;
2) EV datasets of Nanshan District (corresponding to IEEE-141) of Shenzhen;
3) Necessary traffic network parameters to support our research in power-traffic coupled system;
4) A supplement material responsive to the paper, which fully details the mathematical derivation of DSSR models (omitted in the manuscript) and supplement the similar simulation results of other EVCSs
